About Smart Family 360

Connecting Ghanaian farmers directly with consumers since 2020

Our Story

Smart Family 360 was founded with a simple mission: to create a direct link between local farmers and consumers, eliminating unnecessary middlemen and ensuring fair prices for both producers and buyers.

Based in Accra with operations across all 16 regions of Ghana, we've built a platform that empowers smallholder farmers while providing urban families with access to fresh, affordable farm products.
